About us

PUCSA is an innovation club in Pwani University where by the students get a platform to learn, present their ideas and innovative thoughts and also engage in field work activities and events. The club not only offers resources like learning nmaterials but also boosts their budding ideas by converting them into invention of something substantial. It also creates a social environment where students with various technology experiences come together and share information with one another.
